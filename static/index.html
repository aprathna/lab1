Art has always been one of Alisa's most avid hobbies aside from eating, and she has dabbled in it from a very young age. She began with anime, a style of Japanese Cartoons from which she drew a great amount of influence, before beginning to paint in high school. Since then she has expanded her endeavors to many other mediums, such as inks and tape. She likes to encorporate art into her every day life, adding little burst of artistic influence to many daily activities.
